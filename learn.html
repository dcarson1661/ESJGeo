Learn to Use ESJ-Geo

Getting Started:

From the ESJGeo Home page you will is a Green Search button(also found at the bottom of this page). Clicking the Search button with take you to the Search page.

 

 

Once you have found your area of interest you will be presented with a link to view the results. Clicking the link will open the results map for your given area of interest

 

 

Once the map is displayed the user can adjust the variables in the left side panel. The results will dynamiclly be displayed on the map.
